Comprehensive Garment Manufacturing Services

At Universal Garments, we offer end-to-end garment manufacturing solutions that transform raw materials into premium-quality fabrics and finished garments. Our state-of-the-art facilities house advanced machinery and skilled technicians who excel in every stage of the manufacturing process.

  • Weaving – We operate modern looms capable of producing a wide range of fabric types, from basic cotton weaves to complex patterns and textures.
  • Knitting – Our knitting units produce seamless, stretchable fabrics perfect for activewear, casual wear, and fashion garments.
  • Dyeing – Advanced dyeing facilities ensure consistent color matching, colorfastness, and compliance with international standards.
  • Finishing – Fabric finishing processes including washing, softening, and special treatments enhance durability and feel.

Our manufacturing capabilities span natural fibers (cotton, linen, silk), synthetic materials (polyester, nylon), and blended fabrics. We work closely with clients to understand their specific requirements, from fabric weight and texture to color specifications and performance characteristics.

With three factories and over 200 skilled workers, we maintain high production volumes while ensuring quality consistency. Whether you need bulk fabric production or custom textile development, our garment manufacturing services deliver results that meet global standards.

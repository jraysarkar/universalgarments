High-Quality Jackets for Every Market

With an annual production capacity of 2,00,000 jackets, we specialize in both bulk and private label orders, ensuring premium quality with global compliance standards.

  • PU Leather Jackets – durable and stylish outerwear.
  • Puffer Jackets – lightweight warmth for winter markets.
  • Denim Jackets – timeless fashion staple.

Our jackets are produced with precision stitching, weather-resistant fabrics, and modern designs to meet the requirements of global fashion and retail brands. With advanced machinery and multi-level quality checks, we ensure every piece meets international buyer expectations.

Premium Trousers in Multiple Fabrics

Our trousers are known for precision tailoring and comfort, with an annual capacity of 2,00,000 pcs. We offer a variety of fits, styles, and fabrics to serve international clients.

  • 100% Cotton for breathable casuals.
  • Nylon & Terry for durability and sportswear.
  • Pure Linen for premium summer wear.

From formal trousers to everyday casuals, we use modern sewing lines, ISO-certified processes, and stringent inspections to ensure consistent output and international buyer satisfaction.

Stylish Shirts with Global Appeal

We produce up to 1,00,000 shirts annually, combining comfort, quality, and style. From casual wear to luxury fashion, our shirts cater to diverse markets.

  • Classic Cotton Shirts – soft, breathable, and durable.
  • Tie-Dye Shirts – bold, trendy, and vibrant.
  • Silk Shirts – luxurious and elegant.

Our shirts undergo strict AQL-standard inspections, ensuring flawless stitching, accurate sizing, and consistency across batches. We partner with brands worldwide for both bulk and private label shirt production.

Custom Apparel – Designed for Your Brand

We help brands bring unique fashion concepts to life with a production capacity of 1,50,000 pcs/year. From design development to final stitching, our team provides end-to-end support.

  • Women’s Wear – Cord Sets, Shirts, Linen Pants, Palazzos, and more.
  • Bespoke Developments – customized styles as per client specifications.
  • Private Label Manufacturing with export-ready documentation.
In-House Capabilities

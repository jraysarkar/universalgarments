Multi-level Quality Checks Across Production

At Universal Garments, quality is not an afterthought—it is built into every step of our manufacturing process. Our in-house Quality Assurance (QA) team works alongside production lines to catch and correct issues in real time, reducing defects and ensuring consistency across every batch.

  • Raw material inspection – We verify fabric quality, color consistency, and compliance with your specifications before cutting.
  • In-process checks – Stitching, finishing, and assembly are monitored at key stages to maintain standards.
  • Final inspection – AQL-based sampling and full inspection options ensure only approved garments reach your warehouse.

Our QA team is trained on international standards and buyer-specific requirements. We document every check and provide clear pass/fail reports, so you have full visibility into the quality of your order before shipment.

With three factories and over 200 skilled workers, we combine scale with precision. Whether you need standard AQL 2.5 inspection or custom quality protocols, our in-house QA team delivers the consistency and transparency that global brands expect from their manufacturing partners.

Textile Research & Development Services

At Universal Garments, our Textile R&D department focuses on innovation, sustainability, and performance. We work with brands to develop new fabric solutions, improve existing materials, and create sustainable textile alternatives that meet evolving market demands.

  • Fabric Development – We develop custom fabric blends, textures, and finishes tailored to your product requirements and performance needs.
  • Sustainable Solutions – Research into eco-friendly materials, recycled fibers, and sustainable production methods to reduce environmental impact.
  • Performance Testing – We test new fabric formulations for durability, comfort, moisture-wicking, UV protection, and other performance characteristics.
  • Prototype Development – From concept to sample, we help you develop and refine fabric prototypes before full-scale production.

Our R&D team collaborates with clients to understand their vision and translate it into viable fabric solutions. Whether you're looking to create a unique fabric for a new product line or improve the sustainability of existing materials, we provide the expertise and resources to make it happen.

With access to advanced testing equipment and a network of fiber suppliers, we help brands stay ahead of trends and meet consumer demands for innovation, quality, and sustainability in textiles.

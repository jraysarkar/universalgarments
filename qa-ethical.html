Responsible Manufacturing

Our Commitment to People & Planet

Universal Garments is committed to ethical labor practices and environmental responsibility. We believe that quality apparel can and should be produced in a way that respects workers, communities, and the environment. Our factories are run with clear policies on fair wages, safe working conditions, and no forced or child labor.

  • Ethical labor – Fair wages, safe workplaces, and respect for workers’ rights across all three factories.
  • Eco-friendly processes – From water and energy use to waste management, we aim to reduce our environmental footprint.
  • Sustainable sourcing – Where possible, we work with materials and suppliers that meet recognized sustainability standards.

We welcome discussions with buyers who have specific codes of conduct or sustainability requirements. Our goal is to align our operations with your values and to provide the transparency you need for reporting and compliance. Responsible, fair, and transparent practices are not optional for us—they are part of how we do business every day.

By choosing Universal Garments, you partner with a manufacturer that takes ethical and sustainable manufacturing seriously, so your brand can stand behind both the quality and the integrity of your supply chain.

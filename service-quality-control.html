Comprehensive Quality Control & Testing Services

Quality is at the heart of everything we do at Universal Garments. Our dedicated quality control and testing department ensures that every fabric and finished garment meets or exceeds international standards before it reaches your warehouse.

  • Strength Testing – We test fabric tensile strength, tear resistance, and seam durability to ensure garments withstand normal wear and washing.
  • Shrinkage Testing – Pre-shrinkage and dimensional stability tests ensure garments maintain their size after washing.
  • Colorfastness Testing – Color retention tests verify that dyes remain stable through washing, light exposure, and perspiration.
  • Visual Inspection – AQL-based sampling and full inspection options catch defects, inconsistencies, and quality issues before shipment.

Our quality control team follows recognized international standards including AQL (Acceptable Quality Level) sampling, ISO guidelines, and buyer-specific requirements. We maintain detailed test reports and quality documentation that you can use for compliance and record-keeping.

Whether you need standard quality checks or custom testing protocols, our quality control services provide the assurance and documentation you need to maintain your brand's reputation for quality.

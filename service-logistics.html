Complete Logistics & Supply Chain Management

Universal Garments provides comprehensive logistics and supply chain services that ensure your orders are packaged, stored, and shipped efficiently to destinations worldwide. Our logistics team coordinates every aspect of the supply chain to meet your delivery timelines.

  • Packaging – Custom packaging solutions protect garments during transit while meeting your branding and presentation requirements.
  • Warehousing – Secure storage facilities with inventory management systems keep your orders organized and ready for dispatch.
  • Global Distribution – We work with trusted shipping partners to deliver orders via sea, air, or land freight to any destination.
  • Documentation – Complete export documentation, customs clearance support, and shipping tracking keep you informed throughout the process.

Our logistics services are designed to minimize delays and reduce costs. We provide shipping quotes, coordinate with freight forwarders, and handle all necessary paperwork to ensure smooth customs clearance. Whether you need FOB, CIF, or door-to-door delivery, we work with you to find the most efficient shipping solution.

With years of experience in international shipping and a network of reliable logistics partners, we ensure your garments reach their destination on time, every time. Our transparent tracking and communication keep you updated at every stage of the shipping process.

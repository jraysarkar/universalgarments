End-to-End Custom Apparel Production

Universal Garments specializes in custom apparel production, transforming your design concepts into finished garments that reflect your brand's identity. Our comprehensive production process covers every stage from pattern development to final packaging.

  • Cutting – Precision cutting using automated pattern machines ensures accurate sizing and minimal fabric waste.
  • Stitching – Modern sewing lines with skilled operators deliver consistent, high-quality seams and finishes.
  • Embroidery – Advanced embroidery machines create intricate designs, logos, and branding elements.
  • Finishing – Final touches including washing, pressing, quality checks, and packaging ensure your garments are ready for retail.

We work with brands of all sizes, from startups to established retailers. Our team collaborates closely with clients throughout the production process, providing updates, samples, and quality reports. Whether you need small batch production or large-scale manufacturing, we adapt our processes to your timeline and requirements.

Our custom apparel production capabilities include men's wear, women's wear, children's clothing, and specialized garments. With flexible minimum order quantities and competitive pricing, we make custom production accessible to brands at every stage of growth.

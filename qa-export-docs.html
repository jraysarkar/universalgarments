Full Transparency & Export-ready Paperwork

Smooth international shipping and customs clearance depend on accurate, complete documentation. At Universal Garments, we prepare and maintain all export-related documents so your consignments move without delay and your records stay audit-ready.

  • Commercial invoices & packing lists – Prepared to your format and destination country requirements.
  • Certificates of origin & compliance – Where required, we support GSP, preferential trade, and buyer-specific certificates.
  • Quality & test reports – We keep and share test reports for fabric and finished goods as per your requirements.

We believe in full buyer transparency. You get visibility into production status, quality results, and shipping details at every stage. Our team is used to working with brands that need traceability and documentation for sustainability, compliance, and supply chain due diligence.

Whether you ship by sea, air, or land, we ensure documentation is complete, consistent, and aligned with your internal systems. This reduces delays at ports and builds long-term confidence in our partnership.

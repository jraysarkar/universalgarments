Open Doors, Full Transparency

We maintain complete transparency by welcoming independent audits and inspections from buyers at any stage of production.

Why We Welcome Audits & Third-party Inspections

Trust is earned through transparency. At Universal Garments, we do not treat audits or buyer visits as an inconvenience—we see them as an opportunity to demonstrate how we work and to align even more closely with your compliance and quality expectations.

  • Pre-production audits – Review our facilities, machinery, and processes before you place an order.
  • During production – Your team or a third-party inspector can visit at any time to check quality and working conditions.
  • Pre-shipment inspection – We support AQL and custom inspection protocols at our warehouse before dispatch.

We are used to working with global brands that require social compliance, quality, and security audits. Our management and floor staff are briefed to cooperate fully with auditors and to provide access to records, production areas, and documentation as needed.

Whether you use your own inspection team or a recognized third-party agency, we coordinate visits, provide necessary documentation, and act on findings to maintain long-term partnerships. Open communication and accountability are at the core of how we operate.
